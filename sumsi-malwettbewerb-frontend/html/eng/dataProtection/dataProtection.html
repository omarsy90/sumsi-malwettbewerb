Data Protection Information according to GDPR

Data Protection Information according to Art. 13 GDPR for the Sumsi Drawing Competition

We treat your personal data confidentially and in accordance with legal regulations. Hereby, we inform you about the processing of your personal data and that of your child, and the data protection rights and claims you are entitled to. The content and extent of data processing are significantly determined by the information you provide in the drawing competition via the online contact form.

What personal data is processed as part of the drawing competition?

We process the personal data that we receive from you as part of the drawing competition via the online contact form. This data includes your personal details and contact information (first and last name, email address) as well as those of your child (first name and age).

What purpose does the processing of your personal data and that of your child serve?

The personal data you provide and that of your child for participating in the drawing competition is exclusively used by Raiffeisen Provincial Bank for conducting and managing the drawing competition. The first name of your child and your email address are needed to determine and notify the winners and are only processed for this purpose.

Who receives the personal data you provide?

Your personal data and that of your child are only accessible to employees of Raiffeisen Provincial Bank Carinthia who handle the drawing competition in order to determine and notify the winners. No other transfer of your data to third parties takes place.

How long is your provided personal data stored?

Your personal data and that of your child will be deleted after the completion of the drawing competition and after determining and notifying the winners. The deletion of your data and that of your child will occur by November 30, 2020, at the latest.

What data protection rights do you have?

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your and your child's stored data, a right to data portability, the right to object to the processing, and a right to revoke consent under the conditions of data protection law. You can send related requests to datenschutz@rbgk.raiffeisen.at. This does not affect your right to lodge a complaint with the competent data protection authority via email at dsb@dsb.gv.at or by mail to Austrian Data Protection Authority, Barichgasse 40-42, 1030 Vienna.
