COOKIES

Our website uses so-called cookies. Cookies are text files that are stored on your device when you visit the website. We use cookies to perform anonymized analyses of website usage. We also use cookies to offer you additional functions on the website, to make interactions with the website easier and to ensure error-free application (e.g., to facilitate navigation on a website or to save your preferences and settings for your next visit).

Required Cookies: Cookies that are necessary for the basic functions of the website, we process based on our legitimate interest to be able to present our content to you, or due to contractual or legal obligations to provide information on our website.

Functional Cookies: Cookies that enable us to analyze the usage of the website, we use based on your consent when using our website.

Marketing Cookies: Cookies that enable us to present advertising tailored to your interests, we also use based on your consent when using our website.

Some cookies remain stored on your device until you delete them. They enable us to recognize your browser on your next visit. Most of the cookies we use are deleted after your visit to our website (so-called session cookies).

Cookies can be blocked, disabled, or deleted. A variety of tools are available for this (including browser controls and settings). You can find information on this in the help section of the web browser you are using. Disabling all cookies we use may, for example, restrict the display of the website.
